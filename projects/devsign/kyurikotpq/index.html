A Goal Digger's Home on the Web

web devsign / branding

Apr - Jun 2020

The Client

Tea Pei Qi is a application developer and designer currently based in Singapore. Her strengths lie in Web Design and Development, but she's also excited about mastering Robotic Process Automation through her apprenticeship at IBM.

The Problem

In preparation for an internship back in 2019, Tea rushed her old website and has not updated it since.

While the site looks alright on smaller screens, its interface was not intuitive enough to navigate through on mobile. Tea also found it hard to update the site's content, fumbling immensely with formatting and deployment on Firebase.

The Goal

Have an awesome-looking website that...

  • Compels potential clients to get in touch for projects
  • Has an intuitive interface for mobile devices
  • Can be updated easily
